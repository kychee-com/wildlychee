FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Is it really $5–20/mo?

Yes. Wild Lychee is open-source software. The $5–20/mo covers Run402 hosting (database, file storage, edge functions, subdomain). There are no per-member fees, no usage charges, and no hidden costs. The price scales with your hosting tier, not your member count.

Do I need technical skills?

Not with Studio. Tell us about your community, and AI builds your portal in minutes. For the DIY template, basic comfort with deploying web apps is helpful — but AI agents can handle that too.

Who owns the data?

You do. Wild Lychee runs on your own Run402 project with your own database. You can export, migrate, or delete at any time. We never touch your data.

Can I migrate from Wild Apricot or Circle?

Yes. Studio can investigate your existing site and migrate your branding, colors, and content structure. Member data can be imported via CSV. See our comparison page for details.

What does “AI-powered” actually mean?

Six optional AI features run on your own API key (OpenAI or Anthropic): content moderation, auto-translation, newsletter drafts, smart onboarding, member insights, and event recaps. You control what runs, when, and at what cost.

Why is it so affordable?

Because Wild Lychee is open source and runs on Run402's serverless infrastructure. There's no sales team, no enterprise licensing, and no per-seat revenue model. You pay for hosting, not for software.
