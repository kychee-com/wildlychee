Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-02

Overview

Wild Lychee is an open-source community portal template operated by Kychee, Inc. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. Wild Lychee portals run on Run402 infrastructure - see the Run402 Privacy Policy for details on infrastructure-level data handling.

Data We Collect

Kychee, Inc. collects the following data through Wild Lychee services:

  • Studio and Pro customers - email address, payment information (processed by Stripe), portal configuration preferences, and communication history related to your build
  • Marketing site visitors - standard web analytics (page views, referral sources) via privacy-respecting analytics, and any information you voluntarily submit through contact forms
  • Open-source users - we collect no data from self-deployed forks. You're on your own infrastructure.

Data We Do Not Collect

Kychee, Inc. does not have access to the data stored in your portal. Your member directory, announcements, forum posts, events, and uploaded files live in your Run402 project database. We cannot read, modify, or delete your portal data.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not train AI models on your data.

Your Portal's Member Data

When you deploy a Wild Lychee portal, your members will provide personal data (names, emails, profile information, etc.). This data is stored in your Run402 project, not by Kychee, Inc. directly.

  • You are the data controller for all member data in your portal
  • Run402 is the data processor providing the infrastructure
  • You are responsible for your portal's compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws
  • You should publish your own privacy policy for your portal's members

How We Use Data

We use the data we collect exclusively for:

  • Service delivery - building and customizing your portal (Studio/Pro)
  • Billing - processing payments through Stripe
  • Communication - responding to support requests and delivering build updates
  • Improvement - understanding how our marketing site and services are used

AI Features and API Keys

Wild Lychee's optional AI features (content moderation, auto-translation, etc.) use your own API keys (OpenAI or Anthropic), stored as secrets in your Run402 project. Kychee, Inc. does not have access to your API keys or to the data processed by those AI services. Your AI usage is governed by the respective provider's terms.

Data Storage and Security

Studio and Pro customer data is stored securely with encryption at rest and in transit. Payment information is handled entirely by Stripe - we never store credit card numbers. Portal data security is provided by Run402's infrastructure (AWS Aurora, S3, CloudFront) with per-project isolation and row-level security.

Third-Party Services

  • Run402 - portal infrastructure (database, hosting, auth). Run402 Privacy Policy
  • Stripe - payment processing for Studio and Pro. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts - typography on the marketing site. Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Wild Lychee services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are building a portal for a community that includes minors, you are responsible for ensuring COPPA compliance in your portal.

Data Retention

We retain Studio and Pro customer data for the duration of your service relationship and for a reasonable period afterward for legal and accounting purposes. Marketing analytics data is retained in aggregate form. Portal data retention is governed by your Run402 lease - see the Run402 Privacy Policy for details.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by Kychee, Inc. at any time. For data in your portal, you have full control through the Run402 API. For Kychee account-level data, contact privacy@kychee.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made. Continued use of Wild Lychee after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Privacy questions? Email privacy@kychee.com.
