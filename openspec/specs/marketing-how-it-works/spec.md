## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: How it works section

The homepage SHALL include a "How it works" section showing three numbered steps: (1) pick a starting template or niche, (2) AI generates your portal, (3) launch on your domain and customize. Each step SHALL have a number, title, and one-line description.

#### Scenario: Three steps render
- **WHEN** the visitor scrolls to the "How it works" section
- **THEN** three numbered steps SHALL be displayed with titles and descriptions

#### Scenario: Steps link to action
- **WHEN** the visitor reads step 3
- **THEN** a CTA link SHALL direct them to the demo or Studio
